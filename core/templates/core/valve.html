VALVES PROCUREMENT SERVICES

Catobi has an Original Equipment Manufacturer partnership relationship with the best Valve manufacturers in the world; HAWA VALVES, INDIA(pvt) and ARGUS MACHINE COMPANY LIMITED, EDMONTON CANADA in Africa. Catobi procures and supplies valves that are tailored to fit our client’s scope as well as offer world class after-sales support services to our customers. All valves within our product range are Type Acceptance Tested and come with TAT certificates. We procure and supply Ball Valves, Globe Valves, Gate Valves, Check Valves, Butterfly Valves, Plug Valves, Instrument (Block and Bleed) Valves etc of class 150#-2500# API 3000-15000, material trim of Carbon Steel (CS) Stainless Steel (SS), Duplex Stainless Steel (DSS), operators of lever, Gear box or Actuator.

VALVES MAINTENANCE SERVICES

Catobi Valve maintenance facility is furnished with state of the art equipment and dedicated/experienced valve specialists to provide cost effective and reliable solutions to your entire valve. malfunctions. We have the capacity and certifications to maintain all kinds of valves (Ball Valves, Globe Valves, Check Valves, and Butterfly Valves, Plug Valves, double Block and Bleed Valves e.t.c) of any pressure class. Having the facility for disassembling and Assembling of valves, we can repair valves.

VALVE INTEGRITY/PRESSURE TEST

Our Valve test benches have been designed and built to ensure efficiency and maximum safety of the operator and supervising engineer for Valve Pressure Testing and Integrity testing, Our Mobile Testing Unit is available for field/site valves pressure testing. These testing systems are capable of testing pressures 150# – 2500#, API3000 –15000. We have the capacity and certification to maintain all types of Valves (Ball Valves, Globe Valve, Gate Valves, Check Valves, Butterfly Valves, Plug Valves, Safety Relief Valve, Instrument (Block & Bleed) Valves, etc.) and of pressure class 150# – 2500#. Having the facility for dis-assembling and assembling of valves, we can repair valves non-critical parts and seals. Calibration: We calibrate and certify all kind of Guages, Recorders and Safety Relief valves etc. on a timely delivery.
