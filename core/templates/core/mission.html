Mission Statement

To continually set the standard for the oil and gas sector of Nigeria’s economy through the constant innovation of world class service delivery and the unwavering pursuit of excellence in this field.

Quality Statement

The management of CNEL is totally committed to quality and so all personnel in all job functions perform their duties in accordance with the requirement of the Quality System standard and are committed to the company’s quality objective in everyday life.

Community Relations

CATOBI NIGERIA ENTERPRISE LIMITED is committed as a matter of policy towards maintaining an improved relationship with communities in the areas of its operations. To this effect, the community development department of the company ensures that adequate compensation is made where necessary to communities where CATOBI NIGERIA ENTERPRISE LIMITED operates.
