Valve Assembly

We do in-country assembly with qualified Engineers in partnership with our OEM (HAWA VALVES), whereby components of the Valves are shipped in parts and the final assembly and associated test is carried out in our Nigeria Facility, we follow our quality procedure to assure quality products for our Customers, we install operators, such as gear box or actuator to valves.

The essence is to minimize transit time required to ship the operators to our parent factory before delivery to Nigeria. By this process, delivery lead-time is minimized extensively.
