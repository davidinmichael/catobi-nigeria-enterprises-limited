Flow Measurement Calibration

Catobi’s calibration of flow measurement instruments covers:

  • Visual inspection of flow meter installation and condition.
  • Verification of instrument tag number and calibration status.
  • Cleaning and preparation of the instrument before calibration.
  • Calibration using a reference master meter or certified calibration equipment.
  • Comparison of measured flow values with standard reference values.
  • Adjustment of meter parameters if deviation exceeds acceptable tolerance.
  • Verification of transmitter output signals (4–20 mA, digital output, etc.).
  • Functional testing and recording of calibration data.

Valve Calibration and Testing

Catobi’s testing and calibration of control valves, safety relief valves, and pressure relief valves covers:

  • Visual inspection of valve body, actuator, and accessories.
  • Verification of valve identification and service conditions.
  • Functional testing of valve opening and closing operation.
  • Seat leakage testing.
  • Calibration of set pressure or control parameters.
  • Adjustment of spring tension or actuator settings.
  • Pressure testing using a calibrated valve test bench.
  • Recording of opening pressure, reseat pressure, and leakage rate.

Overhead Crane Inspection and Recertification

Catobi’s inspection and recertification of overhead cranes covers:

  • Visual inspection of crane structure and components.
  • Examination of hoist mechanism, wire ropes and chains, hooks and safety latches, sheaves and drums.
  • Electrical system inspection.
  • Load testing using certified test weights.
  • Functional testing of limit switches, brakes, and emergency stop system.
  • Structural integrity assessment.

Forklift Inspection and Recertification

Catobi’s inspection and recertification of forklifts covers the following activities:

  • Visual inspection of the forklift structure.
  • Inspection of lifting components: Mast, Forks, Chains, and Hydraulic cylinders.
  • Functional testing of lifting and lowering mechanisms.
  • Inspection of steering and braking systems.
  • Verification of load capacity plate.
  • Operational safety checks.

Calibration and Recertification of Other Oil & Gas Equipment

Catobi provides calibration and inspection services for various instruments and accessories, including:

  • Pressure gauges
  • Temperature transmitters
  • Level transmitters
  • Gas detectors
  • Torque tools
  • Lifting accessories (slings, shackles, hooks, etc.)
