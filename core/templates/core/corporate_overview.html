Corporate Overview

A company registered and incorporated in Nigeria with 100% indigenous share holding of one million naira share capital, registered under the law of federal republic of Nigeria as a private limited liability company in a decentralize field of oil and gas services, procurement, safety quality control, inspection, Electrical and instrumentation system.

CATOBI NIGERIA ENTERPRISE LIMITED is structured to provide world class services to allied industries, Government entities including Oil and Gas services up-stream and downstream sectors through the provision of best practice of procurement, valves and flanges, safety quality control, inspection, Electrical and Instrumentation system.

CATOBI NIGERIA ENTERPRISE LIMITED was incorporated on the 19th July 2007 and since then it has been consistence in providing quality and safe services to its client.

CATOBI NIGERIA ENTERPRISE LIMITED is a knowledge driven company with assemblage of competent, dedicated and resourceful team of men and women managing the company affairs with oil and gas services, procurement of valves and flanges, safety quality control, inspection, Electrical and instrumentation system. We apply our knowledge, skills and experience to provide solutions and systems that will optimize the safety and integrity of client resources /facilities.
