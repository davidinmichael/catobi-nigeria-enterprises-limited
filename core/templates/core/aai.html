Fabric Maintenance & Corrosion Control Services

Catobi delivers comprehensive fabric maintenance and corrosion protection solutions, including:

  • Abrasive blasting (SA 2.5 / SA 3 standards)
  • Mechanical surface preparation & rust removal
  • Application of protective coating systems (Epoxy, Polyurethane, Zinc-rich primers)
  • Airless spray, brush & roller applications
  • Dry Film Thickness (DFT) measurement & holiday testing
  • Coating inspection & QA/QC documentation
  • Maintenance painting during shutdowns and live plant conditions

Structural Integrity & Minor Steel Repairs

Our engineering team supports:

  • Structural steel repairs and reinforcement
  • Corroded steel member replacement
  • Minor fabrication & welding works
  • Grating, handrails & platform restoration
  • Pipe supports and rack maintenance

Scaffolding, Access & Project Execution Support

Catobi provides:

  • Scaffolding erection & dismantling
  • Rope access solutions (where applicable)
  • Containment systems for blasting & painting
  • Shutdown planning & execution support
  • HSE-compliant workforce supervision

Inspection, QA/QC & Compliance Services

Through our independent inspection capabilities, Catobi ensures:

  • Surface profile inspection
  • Coating system verification
  • NDT services (UT, MPI, DPI where required)
  • Third-party quality assurance
  • Compliance with international standards (ISO, NACE, SSPC where applicable)
  • Detailed reporting & documentation control

Engineering & Technical Support Capability

Backed by our broader engineering strength, Catobi offers:

  • Structural, Mechanical & Civil Engineering support
  • Construction supervision
  • Maintenance planning & documentation
  • Technical workforce training in coating inspection & maintenance
